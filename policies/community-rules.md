# Community Rules

**Status:** DRAFT — UNAPPROVED  
**Effective when:** counsel + product + T&S accept; until then STAGING/INTERNAL only  
**Updated:** 2026-07-20

Adults only (18+). These rules are **behavior-focused**. They are not a “no rules” service and not a vague “offensive speech” code.

## Consent and contact

- Private messaging begins only after a **mutual match**, except tightly rate-limited, non-media interest signals the product explicitly allows.
- Stop contact immediately after block, unmatched withdrawal of consent, or clear “do not contact.”
- Do not coerce, pressure, or retaliate against someone who declines, blocks, or reports.

## Prohibited conduct

### Child safety and age
- No person under 18 may create, access, appear in, or be targeted through the dating service.
- Prohibited: age evasion, grooming, child sexual abuse/exploitation material (CSAM/CSAE), sexualization of minors, sextortion involving minors.

### Trafficking and commercial sexual exploitation
- Prohibited: trafficking, coercion, recruitment for exploitation, pimping, and unlawful commercial-sex solicitation.
- The service must not be used to promote or facilitate prostitution or sex trafficking.

### Intimate imagery and NCII
- Prohibited: nonconsensual intimate imagery (NCII), hidden-camera content, sexual content involving an incapacitated person, threats to distribute intimate material.
- Qualifying NCII removal requests follow the published process; covered platforms must act within applicable legal timelines (including **48 hours** under TAKE IT DOWN Act expectations where applicable). Process is human-owned.

### Stalking, location, and privacy abuse
- Prohibited: stalking, doxxing, disclosing exact location, real-time proximity targeting, contact after block, coercive control.

### Violence and threats
- Prohibited: credible threats, incitement of imminent violence, extortion, kidnapping, instructions tied to an active target.

### Fraud and inauthentic behavior
- Prohibited: impersonation, romance scams, financial fraud, malicious links, credential theft, spam, coordinated inauthentic behavior.

### Platform integrity
- Prohibited: malware, attempts to bypass age, abuse-prevention, block/report, or child-safety controls.

## Public profiles and distribution fitness

Public profiles must remain compatible with Apple and Google Play rules:

- no pornographic primary purpose;
- no explicit public nudity as the product’s purpose;
- no anonymous/random-chat positioning;
- no public “hot-or-not” / attractiveness scoring;
- objectionable public material must be filterable/reportable.

Swipes are **private preference signals**, never public ratings.

## Reporting, blocking, and appeals

- Blocking and reporting are free and available without a paid plan.
- Automation may prioritize, rate-limit, or temporarily contain urgent risk.
- Permanent bans and similarly consequential actions require policy-based **human review**, except where an immediate legal or security block is mandatory.
- Appeals: use the in-product or published channel; bad-faith mass appeals may be limited.

## Enforcement

Responses may include warning, content/profile limits, temporary containment, account suspension, permanent ban, evidence preservation, and referral to appropriate authorities when legally required. Decentralization does **not** remove operator duties for discovery, app distribution, and safety systems we control.

## Deletion and decentralization limits

- You may delete your account and request deletion of operator-held data per the privacy notice.
- Cryptographic and peer limits: the operator cannot force an untrusted peer to erase screenshots or plaintext already received.
- Report evidence and legal holds follow separate retention rules.

## Safety limitations (plain language)

Safety tools reduce risk but **cannot** guarantee identity, prevent screenshots, erase all remote copies, ensure police response, or make an in-person meeting safe.
