# Community Rules (Draft — UNAPPROVED)

Adults only (18+). These rules are behavior-focused. They are **not** a “no rules” or viewpoint-policing policy.

## Prohibited

- minors, age evasion, grooming, CSAM/CSE, sextortion, sexualization of minors;
- trafficking, coercion, exploitation recruitment, pimping, unlawful commercial-sex solicitation;
- nonconsensual intimate imagery, hidden-camera content, sexual content involving an incapacitated person, threats to distribute intimate material;
- stalking, doxxing, exact-location exposure, contact after block, coercive control;
- credible threats, incitement of imminent violence, extortion, kidnapping;
- impersonation, romance scams, fraud, credential theft, spam, coordinated inauthentic behavior;
- harassment after consent withdrawal or block;
- illegal goods/services, malware, bypass of child-safety or abuse-prevention controls.

Public profiles must remain compatible with Apple/Google distribution rules: no pornographic primary purpose, no explicit public nudity, no anonymous/random-chat positioning, no public attractiveness scoring.
