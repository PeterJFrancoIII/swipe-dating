# Community Rules

**Status:** DRAFT — UNAPPROVED  
**Effective when:** counsel + product + T&S accept; until then STAGING/INTERNAL only  
**Updated:** 2026-07-21

Adults only (18+). These rules are **behavior-focused**. They are not a “no rules” service and not a vague “offensive speech” code.

## Consent and contact

- Private messaging begins only after an authenticated **mutual match**, except tightly rate-limited, non-media interest signals the product explicitly allows.
- Stop contact immediately after block, unmatch, withdrawal of consent, or a clear “do not contact.”
- Do not coerce, pressure, shame, threaten, or retaliate against someone who declines, blocks, reports, refuses proximity disclosure, or refuses location sharing.
- Consent to match, message, proximity, sexual activity, group participation, profile disclosure, and location are separate. Consent to one is not consent to the others.

## Child safety and age

- No person under 18 may create, access, appear in, or be targeted through the dating service.
- Minors may not use proximity, sexual-intent modes, maps, messaging, group encounters, or marketplace social features.
- Prohibited: age evasion, grooming, child sexual abuse/exploitation material (CSAM/CSAE), sexualization of minors, sextortion involving minors, and helping another person bypass the adult gate.
- Report suspected minor access immediately. Do not solicit additional evidence or conduct your own investigation.

## Proximity — “Get fk'd”

- The mode is opt-in and adult-only.
- Do not harvest, replay, correlate, sell, or publish Bluetooth encounter identifiers.
- Do not follow, confront, photograph, identify, or repeatedly alert someone because a nearby encounter occurred.
- Do not use modified clients, radio equipment, or coordinated scanning to map a venue or determine who is nearby.
- A proximity alert is not consent to profile disclosure, messaging, sex, touch, following, or location sharing.
- Blocking, emergency privacy, and a “do not contact” request override all proximity behavior.
- Gender never grants permission to reveal a profile automatically. Each user controls their own disclosure policy.

## Location and maps

- Matching never shares location automatically.
- Do not pressure anyone to share an approximate area, meeting pin, or live location.
- Do not forward, screenshot, publish, infer, or use a location grant for surveillance, stalking, coercion, retaliation, or surprise contact.
- Do not attempt to defeat expiry, revocation, approximate-location fuzzing, or blocked-user suppression.
- Location access ends when the grant expires, the sender stops it, the users unmatch, or either user blocks the other.

## Looking For and sexual conduct

- Sexual intentions are private adult compatibility signals—not a public broadcast and not consent to sexual activity.
- “Sex,” “Group sex,” non-monogamy, BDSM/kink, and other adult modes require ongoing, specific consent.
- Group encounters require every participant to know the current participant list and affirmatively accept it. Adding a person requires renewed consent from everyone.
- No sexual services marketplace, trafficking, coercion, recruitment for exploitation, pimping, or unlawful commercial-sex solicitation.
- No stealthing, reproductive coercion, drugging, sexual activity involving an incapacitated person, or recording/distribution without consent.

## Intimate imagery and NCII

- Prohibited: nonconsensual intimate imagery (NCII), hidden-camera content, sexual content involving an incapacitated person, threats to distribute intimate material, and deceptive sexual deepfakes where prohibited.
- Qualifying NCII requests follow the published human-owned process and applicable legal timelines.

## Stalking, privacy abuse, and violence

- Prohibited: stalking, doxxing, exact-location exposure, contact after block, coercive control, credible threats, incitement of imminent violence, extortion, kidnapping, and instructions tied to an active target.
- Do not combine profile, proximity, map, marketplace, or questionnaire information to identify or target someone outside the service.

## Fraud, bots, and platform integrity

- Prohibited: impersonation, romance scams, financial fraud, malicious links, credential theft, spam, bot farms, scripted swiping, mass profile scraping, account farms, coordinated inauthentic behavior, and report brigading.
- Do not bypass passkeys, adult credentials, App Attest, Play Integrity, request challenges, rate limits, replay controls, block/report systems, or child-safety controls.
- Security challenges and rate limits may not be sold or bypassed through payment.
- Permanent bans and similarly consequential actions require policy-based human review except where an immediate legal or security containment is mandatory.

## Skin Shop and creator content

- Only upload assets you created or have permission to use.
- Prohibited: stolen art, impersonation, trademark abuse, malware, executable payloads, hidden network calls, deceptive purchase flows, extremist merchandise, CSAM/CSAE, NCII, and assets that target protected groups with hate or harassment.
- Avatars must not falsely claim photo verification or impersonate another real person.
- Marketplace purchases never improve dating reach, ranking, visibility, moderation outcomes, or safety access.
- Copyright, refund, creator suspension, and appeal processes remain human-owned.

## Preferences and alignment

- Users may privately select genders, intentions, lifestyle preferences, grooming compatibility, and questionnaire answers for their own feed.
- Prohibited: public negative labels about excluded identities, discriminatory harassment, and ranking/filtering by race, skin color, ethnicity, disability, height, spending, or inferred protected/sensitive traits.
- Do not scrape, expose, sell, or use another person’s political, sexual, health, relationship, or questionnaire answers outside the consented dating context.
- Compatibility scores are estimates, not proof of character, truth, safety, intelligence, or relationship success.

## Public profiles and distribution fitness

Public profiles must remain compatible with Apple and Google Play rules:

- no pornographic primary purpose;
- no explicit public nudity as the product’s purpose;
- no anonymous/random-chat positioning;
- no public “hot-or-not” or attractiveness scoring;
- objectionable public material must be filterable and reportable.

Swipes are **private preference signals**, never public ratings.

## Reporting, blocking, and appeals

- Blocking, reporting, emergency privacy, location revocation, and account deletion are free and available without a paid plan.
- Reports may cover proximity stalking, location coercion, group-consent violations, marketplace abuse, suspected minors, NCII, scams, threats, and other prohibited conduct.
- Automation may prioritize, rate-limit, challenge, or temporarily contain urgent risk.
- Permanent bans and similarly consequential actions require policy-based **human review**, except where an immediate legal or security block is mandatory.
- Appeals use the in-product or published channel; bad-faith mass appeals may be limited.

## Enforcement

Responses may include warning, content/profile limits, proximity or location disablement, marketplace removal, temporary containment, account suspension, permanent ban, evidence preservation, payout hold, and referral to appropriate authorities when legally required. Decentralization does **not** remove operator duties for discovery, app distribution, marketplace, and safety systems we control.

## Deletion and decentralization limits

- You may delete your account and request deletion of operator-held data per the privacy notice.
- The operator cannot force an untrusted peer to erase screenshots, plaintext, exported assets, or content already received.
- Bluetooth identifiers and location grants are designed to expire, but compromised or modified devices may retain observations.
- Report evidence, payment records, fraud records, and legal holds follow separate retention rules.

## Safety limitations

Safety tools reduce risk but **cannot** guarantee identity, prevent screenshots, erase all remote copies, ensure Bluetooth delivery, prevent radio tracking by a determined attacker, ensure police response, or make an in-person or group meeting safe.
