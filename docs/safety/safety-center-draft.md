## Our commitment (draft)

Swipe Dating is for **adults 18+** only. We design for mutual consent, privacy, and safety:

- Block and report are always free — never behind a paywall
- We do not sell dating, sexuality, or message content for advertising
- Operators cannot read your ordinary profile, photos, or messages
- Location is coarse-region only — never exact GPS to other users

If someone makes you feel unsafe, use in-app **Report**. For imminent danger, contact local emergency services first.
