## Product floor

- Hard 18+ age floor; no parental-consent bypass.
- Fail closed when eligibility cannot be established.
- Public CSAE prohibition in community rules and store listings when public.
- In-app reporting; action after actual knowledge of CSAM; preservation/reporting per counsel-approved process (e.g. 18 U.S.C. § 2258A considerations).
- Named child-safety contact before public beta: `CHANGE_ME`.

## Agent prohibitions

- Do not create realistic CSAM test fixtures.
- Do not send NCMEC CyberTipline or law-enforcement reports.
- Do not ingest real child-safety evidence into dev environments.
