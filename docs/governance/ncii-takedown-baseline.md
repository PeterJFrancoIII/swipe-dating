# NCII / TAKE IT DOWN baseline

**Status:** DRAFT — UNAPPROVED — not legal advice  
**Updated:** 2026-07-20

Covered platforms are expected to provide an easy process for nonconsensual intimate imagery (NCII) removal requests and to remove qualifying content and known identical copies within **48 hours** where the TAKE IT DOWN Act and FTC guidance apply.

## Staging requirements

- Documented request channel for users and non-users (`CHANGE_ME` before public beta).
- Case tracking IDs; no ordinary staff access to evidence vault.
- Playbooks in `docs/safety/ncii-playbook.md`.
- Human-owned adjudication; agents must not send real notices or handle real intimate evidence.

## Honest limits

Peer-held copies and screenshots may persist outside operator control; disclose this in privacy and safety copy.
