# Funding and impact-claim gates

**Status:** DRAFT — UNAPPROVED  
**Updated:** 2026-07-20

Intent to support life-saving measures for women and children requires financial discipline.

## Before any public impact/funding claim

- [ ] Proper legal entity and charity/commercial relationship advice obtained
- [ ] Allocation policy published (definition of net revenue exact)
- [ ] Segregated accounts and conflict-of-interest policy
- [ ] Independent review path for grants/beneficiaries
- [ ] No donor/sponsor access to user data or safety cases
- [ ] No paywall on safety; no sale of sensitive data; no behavioral ads on dating data

## Forbidden claims

- Guaranteeing lives saved or rescue by the app
- Unsubstantiated charitable percentages
- Using beneficiary stories without informed consent and safeguarding review
