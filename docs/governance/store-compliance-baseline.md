# App Store & Play compliance baseline

**Status:** DRAFT — UNAPPROVED — re-verify URLs ≤30 days before submission  
**Updated:** 2026-07-20

## Apple (UGC / dating-relevant)

Must support:

- filtering or prevention of objectionable public content;
- report abusive content; timely action;
- block abusive users;
- published developer contact information;
- age-appropriate access / declared age range where required;
- no pornographic primary purpose;
- no random/anonymous-chat positioning;
- no public hot-or-not / objectification;
- account deletion and accurate privacy labels.

Sources to re-read before submit: [App Review Guidelines](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/review/guidelines/).

## Google Play (UGC / dating / child safety)

Must support:

- terms acceptance before UGC upload;
- defined prohibited behavior;
- ongoing moderation;
- in-app report and block;
- robust age gating for dating/matchmaking (simple circumvention-prone gates may be insufficient);
- Child Safety Standards: public CSAE standards, in-app reporting, action after actual knowledge of CSAM, legal reporting processes, child-safety point of contact;
- accurate Data safety form.

Sources to re-read before submit: [Play Developer Policy](https://play.google.com/about/developer-content-policy/).

## Product implications

- Swipes are private, not public scores.
- Safety features are free.
- Public profiles stay distribution-compatible.
